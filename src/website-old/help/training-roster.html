Record a training roster

Recording a roster requires at least trainingeditor permissions.

Create the training event
  1. In the left menu, under "Training", click “Rosters”
  2. At the top of the list, click “New ...”

    This should open a new window:
  3. Enter name of training or meeting.
  4. Enter the location of the training.
  5. Enter the name of the (Washington) county in which the training took place.
  6. Enter DEM from county, or leave blank.
  7. Enter the time the training started.
  8. Enter the time the training ended.
  9. At a training event there may be an opportunity for participants to complete one or more training components:
    1. Click the first course to select it.
    2. If additional courses were offered, hold the Ctrl key while clicking to add additional courses.
    3. If you click a course by mistake, hold the Ctrl key while clicking it again to unselect it.
  10. Click Save

    11. You should now be looking at a blank roster form:
